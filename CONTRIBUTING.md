# Contribution Guidelines

This is a list of significant cases of mistakes made by GenAI and humans trusting its output. It is not for theoretical AI dangers, other AI technologies such as self-driving cars, or cases of GenAI being used intentionally for evil purposes. If you would like to contribute to such a list, I recommend checking out [daviddao/awful-ai](https://github.com/daviddao/awful-ai) as well as [KonBriefing’s AI Incident List](https://konbriefing.com/ai/en/ai-incidents.html).

Ensure that your contribution adheres to the following guidelines.

## General guidelines

- Search previous suggestions before making a new one, as yours may be a duplicate.
- Ensure that your editor is configured to remove trailing whitespace.
- Check your spelling and grammar.
- Make a separate pull request for each suggestion.
- Your commit and pull request should have descriptive titles.

## Additional guidelines for adding a new item

- Maintain the descending chronological order of the list items. Add the new item in the correct spot, and not simply at the end of the section.
- Link to the most reputable source available for the incident or news story.
- Use an en-dash character (`–`) to separate the link from the description.
- Use typographic quotes (`“”`) and apostrophes (`’`).
- Sentences need to end with periods.
